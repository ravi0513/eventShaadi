Eventshaadi specializes in making your events truly spectacular through
our extensive experience in event management and wedding planning.

Eventshaadi.is is a premier event management and wedding planning company headquartered in Delhi. Our mission is to offer a comprehensive range of event management services to cater to both corporate and personal occasions. From live shows and celebrity events to entertainment galas, product launches, themed parties, weddings, birthdays, fashion shows, ghazal nights, and beyond, we specialize in creating memorable experiences for every type of event imaginable..

With over a decade of dedicated experience in the event management and wedding planning industry, our team of highly trained and skilled event planners possesses the expertise needed to elevate your event to new heights. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional services at competitive rates, ensuring 100% client satisfaction. From out-of-the-box concepts to innovative solutions, we strive to make your event truly unforgettable.
